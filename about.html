WE ARE LA CASA DE LA FRUTA

Our store has been founded in 2000 with a single intention of providing quality products to coustomers. Even though our store is located in Australia, you can still order from us through our website and it will reach your doorway at the time you specify inside your country. We also have a physical store if customers want to check out our products in person.

Even though our store name means “House of Fruits”, we also have the widest assortments of vegetables in diverse categories.

We provide you services like:

  • Wider selection of product range
  • Spectacular buying experience
  • Allow you to choose the time of delivery apt for your convenience
  • On-time delivery of goods
  • Easier user interface to order goods
  • Valuable customer service whenever needed.

OUR CEO

‘La Casa de La Fruta’ was a mango stall founded in 2000 by Charles Johnson where he sold freshly picked mangoes from his garden in Australia. As his business grew he started selling other fruits and vegetables. However, technology has become more advance over the years, causing profits to decrease as competition started rising. Charles shifted his store online, expanding his customer reach. Now, La Casa de La Fruta ships fresh fruits and vegetables from Australia to more that 30 countries around the world.
